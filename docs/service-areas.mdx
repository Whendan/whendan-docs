# Service Areas

As a dispatcher, you manage operations within specific geographic zones called service areas. Service areas define where your team can pick up and deliver packages, helping you optimize routes and ensure efficient coverage. 
Running a global OpenRouteService (ORS) instance isn’t practical for most businesses. Instead, we use service areas to keep your operations focused and manageable.


## What is a Service Area?

A service area sets the boundaries for where your services are available. This helps you:

- Assign drivers to the right zones
- Prevent out-of-area deliveries
- Plan routes more efficiently

You can create a service area in two ways:

1. Draw on the map: Use our interactive map tool to outline your service area. This is perfect for custom zones that don’t match city or district lines.
2. Import from GeoJSON: Already have boundaries defined? Import them as a GeoJSON file to set up your service area instantly.
