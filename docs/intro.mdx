# What Is WhenDan

WhenDan is an open source logistics operating system designed to streamline management, optimization, and day-to-day operations for transport teams.

It brings package tracking, fleet coordination, warehouse operations, and route optimization into one platform so teams can ship faster with fewer manual handoffs.

## Why Choose WhenDan

- **Developer Friendly**: Built on Supabase and a modern Next.js stack for fast iteration and clear data workflows.
- **Security First**: Authentication plus role and permission-aware access patterns for safer operations.
- **Open Source**: Deploy in cloud or on-premise based on your operational and compliance needs.
- **Smart Assignment and Dispatch Support**: Use dashboard workflows and optimization data to assign work more effectively.

## Why It Exists

WhenDan was born from real transport operations where teams still relied on paper at critical points.

Those workflows caused repeated inefficiencies:

- Suboptimal routing and underused equipment
- Rising fuel and labor waste
- Delays and avoidable delivery errors

WhenDan is focused on reducing that waste with better planning, visibility, and execution.

## Current Scope

The current platform includes:

- Package management and timeline tracking
- Fleet and driver coordination workflows
- Warehouse service flows
- Route and optimization-related data models
- Shipping label generation
